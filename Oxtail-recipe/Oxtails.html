Jamaican Oxtails

Flavorful Oxtails

Oxtails are a popular Jamaican dish that is delicious and tender. Great for Sunday dinner!

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs of Oxtails
  • 1 tsp All Purpose Seasoning
  • 1 can of Butter Beans
  • 1 tsp of Blue Mountain Oxtail Seasoning

Steps

  1. Mix all your ingredients with your oxtails
  2. Brown the oxtails for 10-15 minutes
  3. Add oxtails to Instant Pot pressure cooker for 45 minutes
  4. Once finished oxtails will be tender and ready to eat!
